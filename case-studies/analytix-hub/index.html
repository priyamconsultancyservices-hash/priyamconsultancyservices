Fast Recruitment & On-Time Payroll Enablement for an IT Company

About Analytix Hub

Analytix Hub is an IT services firm delivering technology solutions and operational support for business scaling needs.

Overview

Analytix Hub needed rapid recruitment and precise payroll management to meet urgent project demands during a critical expansion.

The Challenge

Facing time-critical hiring needs and payroll accuracy demands, the company needed to reduce the time-to-hire and ensure payroll precision without delaying project timelines.

Our Strategy

  • Conducted simultaneous recruitment and payroll management
  • Identified, screened, and onboarded candidates within just 7 days, reducing typical hiring time by 40%
  • Ensured accurate payroll processing without delays
  • Maintained operational continuity during rapid expansion
  • Reduced turnover time by 35%, enabling faster project ramp-up

The Result

PCS helped Analytix Hub scale its workforce efficiently, reducing hiring turnaround by 40% and ensuring timely payroll. The company achieved smoother onboarding, improved efficiency, and uninterrupted project operations.
